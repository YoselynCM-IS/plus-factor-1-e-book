Simple Present Tense

 

 

We use Simple Present

 

•
For habits and actions that we do regularly:

 

He visits his friends every Sunday.

 

She goes to school by bus.

 

•
For general truths: The sun rises in the East.

 

•
For permanent situations: He lives in Athens.

Simple Present Spelling Rules

 

We add –s to the form of the verb in most cases when we refer to he, she or it

 

Example:

 

love loves  
He loves his job.

 

read reads  
He reads a novel.

 

walk walks  
He walks to the bus stop.
