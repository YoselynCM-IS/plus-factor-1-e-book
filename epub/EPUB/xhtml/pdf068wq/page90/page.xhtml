How much do you use technology?

 

How much do you know about technology?  

 

Do you enjoy it?

 

Share with your classmates the technology you use in

 

your daily life.

 

In this unit you will

 

•
Use verb  
to be 
in present simple.

 

•
Use adjectives to describe gadgets.

 

•
Learn comparative adjectives.

 

•
Learn superlative adjectives.

 

•
Describe a gadget.

 

Learning outcome

 

•
Students work in teams to use verb  
to be 
in simple present and adjectives to describe  

 

three gadgets.

 

•
Students write two comparative sentences in negative form and two superlative

 

sentences that help to explain which of the three gadgets described is the one that

 

best adapts to their needs.

 

Learning evidence

 

•
Seven sentences describing and comparing gadgets to be uploaded

 

to an online blog.
