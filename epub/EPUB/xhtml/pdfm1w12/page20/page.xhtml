Darwin. It’s in the north of Australia. Darwin is a modern city. It is rainy and dry, too.

 

I am a wholesaler. I love my job. I frequently visit countries in different continents to

 

contact manufacturers to get the best prices of products I sell to small companies. I am

 

married. My husband’s name is George. He is a loving man and he helps me with my

 

business. We have a baby. Her name is Chloe. She’s nine months old.
