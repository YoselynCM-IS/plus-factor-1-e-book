Say hi!

 

Is it easy for you to say hello to someone you don’t know?

 

We say hello in many different ways. It all depends on

 

the person we are greeting.

 

What style do you prefer?

 

In this unit you will:

 

•
Learn different ways to greet people and introduce yourself.

 

• Review the alphabet sounds.

 

• Learn nationalities and countries.

 

• Review numbers and practice them in simple calculations.

 

• Design a questionnaire to get important information from people.

 

Learning outcome

 

• Identifies and describes his/her way to say Hello and his/her classmates’ way to say Hello.

 

•
Learns formal and informal greetings.

 

•
Identifies nationalities and countries.

 

•
Uses verb to be in present simple, in its interrogative form to get information from other

 

people.

 

Learning evidence

 

• Writes and uploads to an online blog a short dialogue in which they use greetings and the

 

questions What's your name? Where are you from? How old are you? and How do you

 

spell...? in order to get to know other individuals.
